Know More About Us

FoodHub is a proudly Nigerian company, operating in the hospitality sectors of the Nigerian economy. The brand is one of the business units operated by Smackers Ltd and has operational presence via multiple outlets, across different states in Nigeria.

Our operations span different aspects of the hospitality sector: Restaurants, Clubs, Lounges and Hotels.

Our goal is to be the most sought after experience within our sectors of play and we are guided by our core values of INNOVATION, QUALITY, UNPARALLELED AMBIENCE & CONSISTENCY
